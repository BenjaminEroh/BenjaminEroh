AB Lite Organization

          Our mission is to empower small villages in Africa by bringing awareness to the lack of sustainable electricity, within their communities. At least 100 million people are still living without access to electricity in Africa in urban areas. In Nigeria, Tanzania, Ghana, and Liberia alone, there are 95 million people alone without electricity, even though they are all within proximity to the grid.

          Urban communities in these areas face many challenges in obtaining electricity access, especially with the  expensive high connection costs to the challenges of informal housing. However, recent advances in renewable energy technology means are more affordable and cleaner alternatives in Africa.Our hope is that we can provide a service which will support these communities needing electricity, so that it may enable their economic growth and infrastructure. Our mission and purpose is to make every urban area in Africa so bright that not one home is without electricity.
